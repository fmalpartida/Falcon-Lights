Welcome to Bandai's Millenium Falcon Light kit.

Welcome to the Millenium Falcon Light kit. We have created this simple LED driver board to retrofit to Bandai's 1:144 Millenium Falcon kit. This board is capable of driving up to 6 white LED from 2 AAAA batteries. The driver is a nice upgrade from the original Bandai Falcon light kit which it's only capable of supporting 2 LEDs.

This driver retrofits nicely into the pre-molded compartment of the original kit and gives you easy access to replace the batteries and ON/OFF switch.
